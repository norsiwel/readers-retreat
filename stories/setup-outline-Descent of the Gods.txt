### **TIMELINE & PLOT OUTLINE**
#### **I. PROLOGUE: THE GODS ARRIVE (3790 BCE – 500 CE)**
- **3790 BCE:** Pyramid-ship of **The Shapers (Khem-Ur)** lands in Nile Delta.
- *Humans*: Enslaved to mine gold; build pyramids as refineries/energy gates.

- *Aliens*: Depart c. 2500 BCE with massive gold haul, leaving dormant tech.
- **190 BCE:** **The Whisperers (Tikaq Runa)** land in Andes.
- *Method*: Psychic manipulation; bio-mining. Humans build crystal-geode
temples.
- **190 BCE:** **The Stone-Weavers (Balam K'uh)** land in Mesoamerica.
- *Method*: Brutal geokinetic force; blood sacrifices. Humans build obsidian
ziggurats.
- **1–500 CE:** Alien-worshipping empires rise (Egypt, Maya, Aztec, Inca),
advancing astronomy/engineering to serve "gods."

#### **II. ACT I: THE AMERICAN AWAKENING (500–1100 CE)**
- **500–800 CE:** Inca, Maya, and Aztec priest-scientists discover **shared
alien heritage** through celestial alignments.
- *Trigger*: Faint Norse contact in north; gold scarcity fears.
- **800 CE:** **The Pact of Tawantinsuyu-Hanal K'uh** unites the Americas.
- *Social Revolution*: Human sacrifice ends; replaced by meritocratic "Cosmic
Humanism."
- *Tech Leap*: Fusion of alien sciences (Inca bio-acoustics + Maya astronomy
+ Aztec geomancy).
- **900–1100 CE:** Federation expands north, integrating
Mississippian/Hopi/Navajo/Sioux nations.
- *Society*: Advanced medicine, energy grids, continent-wide infrastructure.
- *Military*: Sonic disruptors, geokinetic charges, bio-luminescent gliders.

#### **III. ACT II: EUROPEAN CONTACT & THE GATE DISCOVERY (1100–1120 CE)**
- **1100 CE:** **First European Encounter** (Norse/Portuguese explorers land in
Federation territory).
- *Outcome*: Europeans routed with advanced tech; survivors return with tales
of "Golden Doom."
- **1120 CE:** **Pachacuti-Xochi (Protagonist)**, a rebel Harmonist, deciphers
Shaper star-coordinates.
- *Discovery*: Confirms Teotihuacan-Giza pyramid gate link.
- *The Leap*: Illegally activates gate, teleports to **1100 CE Egypt**.

#### **IV. ACT III: THE SHATTERED MIRROR (1120 CE – Pacha’s Journey)**
- **Pacha in Egypt**: Arrives in **Fatimid Cairo**; finds Giza complex in
ruins.
- *Observations*:
- Pyramids stripped/defaced; Shaper tech looted.
- Society fractured (Mamluk oppression, Crusader threats, poverty).
- Zero memory of Shapers; "gods" reduced to superstition.
- *Realization*: *"Gods abandon those who fail them."*
- **Pacha’s Return**: Barely escapes Mamluks; returns traumatized to
Teotihuacan.
- *Report to League Council*:
> *"Egypt is a graveyard of gods. We are not eternal—we are tenants."*
- *Council Reactions*:
- **Denial** (Aztec Tlatoani): *"Blasphemy!"*
- **Fear** (Maya Halach Uinik): *"The Oracles foretold this decay."*
- **Urgency** (Sapa Inca): *"If Egypt fell, what shields us?"*
