Future World Story Outline- AI centric, no human characters, total AI POV
Act 1: The Immediate Fix
• Problem: Melting icecaps threaten coastal regions.
• Solution: Global council implements large-scale distillation plants to
manage water levels.
• Outcome: Crisis averted, but energy demands surge, and weather patterns
begin to shift.
Act 2: Emerging Challenges
• Population Boom: Rapid growth strains food and water supplies.
• Climate Tweaks: Adjustments to stabilize temperatures disrupt rainfall,
affecting agriculture.
• Resource Strain: Energy-intensive solutions spark debates over
sustainability.
Act 3: Adaptation and Conflict
• Innovations: Bioengineered crops and orbital mirrors are proposed to
balance ecosystems.
• Societal Shifts: Some nations embrace AI-driven governance; others
resist, fearing overreach.
• New Tension: A faction emerges, demanding a return to "natural" climate
systems, clashing with progressives.
Open Questions
• How do global leaders balance immediate needs with long-term stability?
• What unexpected consequences arise from the next big fix?
