﻿The imposing bank's architecture stood as a testament to a future that Cody
Darkstar had once envisioned—futuristic, sleek, and dazzling. The building

initials on page after page.
The loan broker's office was a symphony of motion and information. Monitors
filled with data, numbers, and symbols adorned the walls. Cody occasionally
caught glimpses of names of distant star systems, their significance now

longer realms of wonder but collateral in a deal.
The banker's motions were swift and mechanical, flipping pages as though
programmed. "Sign here, and here," he instructed, pointing to each page with a
