The council meetings were tense affairs now. No longer discussions about

I looked towards Leora, now known as the Oracle - though I doubted she much

gaze distant and unfocused. The ichor-tech, meant to heal and protect, had

I saw it in her face: that terrible burden, the crushing weight of

land itself.

It wasn't an easy decision to rally Athens and other city-states against Crete.
Many saw our coalition as reckless aggression, driven by fear rather than
reason. But I knew we couldn’t stand idly by while the Mediterranean withered

I quickly sought out Leora, hoping her wisdom and intuition could provide some
